About Us

We are a dedicated team of volunteers organizing a soccer tournament within the Djiboutian community in Montreal. Our main goal is to create a sporting event that brings together community members, thereby fostering social cohesion and strengthening the bonds among us.

About Sport in our community

Sport plays a crucial role within the Djiboutian diaspora in Montreal, promoting not only health and well-being but also social cohesion and cultural integration. Members of the Djiboutian community in Montreal organize around various sports to maintain strong ties among themselves and with their cultural roots.

The most popular sport among the Djiboutian diaspora in Montreal is football, known as soccer in North America. Football, in particular, holds a central place. Informal tournaments and community leagues are frequently organized by our community in Montreal, allowing both youth and adults to gather regularly.

About Sport in our community

Sport within the Djiboutian diaspora in Montreal is much more than just a physical activity. It is a vehicle for social bonding, cultural expression, and integration, playing a crucial role in the daily lives of Djiboutians in Montreal. Through dynamic community initiatives and active participation, sport helps to strengthen the social fabric of this diaspora while celebrating and preserving Djiboutian cultural roots.
