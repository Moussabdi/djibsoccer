About Sport in our community

Sport within the Djiboutian diaspora in Montreal is much more than just a physical activity. It is a vehicle for social bonding, cultural expression, and integration, playing a crucial role in the daily lives of Djiboutians in Montreal. Through dynamic community initiatives and active participation, sport helps to strengthen the social fabric of this diaspora while celebrating and preserving Djiboutian cultural roots.
